## The Brief

Design a Croctober 2026 campaign that delivers *comfort you feel, not just comfort you wear* — extending Crocs' "Wonderfully Unordinary" platform into an emotionally resonant, digitally-native activation.

---

## The Idea: Croc Tears

*Crocodile tears are fake. Yours are the good kind.*

"Crocodile tears" is a phrase every culture knows — it means fake emotion. But Crocs has spent a decade proving that comfort lets people be real. This campaign reclaims the phrase: Croc tears aren't fake. They're the tears you cry when you're comfortable enough to feel.

**The activation:** A digital-first microsite at crocs.com/croctears where fans answer one question — *"What gives you the good happy kind of tears?"* Each answer becomes one dot. 23,000 dots, over 22 days, collectively reveal a hidden Crocs Classic Clog. The shoe doesn't exist until the fans build it — one feeling at a time.

---

## Why Only Crocs Can Own This

- **The name is the idea.** No competitor can steal "Croc tears" — it's built from the brand name itself, claiming a 600-year-old cultural phrase permanently
- **Answers the brief directly.** The shoe barely appears until Day 22. The entire experience is emotional comfort, not product marketing
- **The fans build the shoe.** The community doesn't just consume the campaign — they create it. The shoe only becomes visible because real people shared real feelings

---

## Campaign Architecture

**Phase 1 — The Reveal (Oct 1–22)**
- Microsite launches with a Crocs shoe hidden behind 23,000 opaque dots
- Each submission removes one dot, revealing a piece of the shoe underneath
- Live progress bar, real-time ticker of global submissions, country tags
- Personal shareable card generated instantly after submission ("croc'd in forever")

**Phase 2 — Croc Day (Oct 23)**
- Fully revealed shoe becomes the hero — chosen by Crocs' team for maximum cultural impact heading into holiday
- 3-minute hero film drops built entirely from real anonymous submissions
- Twins matching goes live: strangers from different countries who submitted similar moments are paired, generating a second viral wave

**Physical Extension**
- 8-foot Crocs sculpture outside SoHo flagship with live LED microsite display
- Designed for organic social amplification from street-level foot traffic
